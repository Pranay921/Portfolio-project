Dr. Nitish Gupta

Ph.D., M.Sc., Department of Mathematics

National Institute of Technology Warangal (NIT Warangal), India

I have completed my Ph.D. in the Department of Mathematics at the National Institute of Technology Warangal (NIT Warangal), located in Telangana, India, under the supervision of Prof. D. Bhargavi. My doctoral research focuses on enhancing heat transfer in porous media under the local thermal non-equilibrium (LTNE) model.

I am currently working as a Postdoctoral Fellow in the School of Basic Sciences at the Indian Institute of Technology Bhubaneswar (IITBBS), India under the supervision of Prof. T.V.S. Sekhar. Prior to this, I served as an Assistant Professor in the School of Chemical Engineering and Physical Sciences at Lovely Professional University (LPU), Punjab (NIRF Rank 27). During my tenure at LPU, I was actively involved in teaching Probability and Statistics, academic mentoring, and curriculum development.
